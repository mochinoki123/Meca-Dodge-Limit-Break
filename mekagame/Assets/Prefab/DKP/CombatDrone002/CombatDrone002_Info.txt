Combat Drone 002 - Unity Asset Package

3D Model + Textures.

To View the Demo Scene:

1. Open the modelDemoScene.unity scene located in the CombatDrone002/Demo/Scenes folder.
2. Play the Demo Scene and Use these Orbit Camera Controls to View the 3D Model:
> Zoom In - E Key
> Zoom Out - Q Key
> Rotate Left - A Key
> Rotate Right - D Key
> Rotate Up - W Key
> Rotate Down - S Key

To use this 3D model in your project:

1. Locate combat drone prefab in the CombatDrone002/Prefabs folder.
2. Drag into your prefered scene.
